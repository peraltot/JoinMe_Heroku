About

JoinME is a social based project tracking platform for people on the go. Stop drop and collaborate. This application scales. We promise.
Starting a project can be tough! But we can increase our progress together! JoinMe allows you to:

  • Enter and track projects/goals.
  • Share with others.
  • Search for active projects.
  • Get notified of user progress.
