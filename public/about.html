About

JoinME is a social based project tracking platform for people on the go who don't want to go it alone. Stop drop and collaborate. This application scales. We promise.

Starting a project can be tough! But we can increase our progress together! For those of us that lose sight of the end goal, JoinME can help you break your goals into manageable steps because small improvements over time lead to stunning results. The project you are most resisting carries your greatest growth. JoinMe allows you to:

  • Enter and track projects/goals.
  • Share with others.
  • Search for active projects to join.
  • Get notified of user progress.
